The Band

Lowfall is an alternative rock band that made its start in Kitsap County. The lead singer is Teo Mommsen, on bass is Daniel Ramos, and Jeff Clearwater as the drummer. All three members were previously in other rock bands that performed in Kitsap County. Lowfall has an amazing stage presence and will rock all night. See below for upcoming shows.
